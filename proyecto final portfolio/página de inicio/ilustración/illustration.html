Romanian K-Pop Demon Hunters

This illustration was inspired by the movie K-pop Demon Hunters and by a trend that went viral on TikTok and Instagram in which artists from all around the world made their own version of the Hunters but with the traditional clothes of their country/region.

Inktober

In 2025 I decided to follow the Inktober list made by Marika Paz (@marikapaz on Instagram) that she called Nestober. For these illustrations I chose to implement each day two characters that I designed (or just one of them, it depends). One is a bear and the other one is a bunny.
